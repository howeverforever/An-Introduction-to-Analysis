Since $E$ is closed, then $E^c$ is open. So for any $a\in E^c$, there exists $r>0$ such that $B_r(a)\subseteq E^c$. For any $x\in E$, since $x\notin B_r(a)$, then $$ \| x-a \| \geq r > 0.$$

\begin{Exercise}
\begin{proof}
Since $E$ is closed, then $E^c$ is open. So for any $a\in E^c$, there exists $r>0$ such that $B_r(a) \subseteq E^c$. For any $x\in E$, since $x\notin B_r(a)$, then
$$
\rho(x,a) \geq r > 0.
$$
It follows that
$$
\inf_{x\in E}\rho(x,a) > 0.
$$
\end{proof}
\end{Exercise}
